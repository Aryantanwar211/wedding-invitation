Our Story

A Tale Written in Time

Every love has a beginning few could have predicted. Here is ours, in four small moments that led to one large promise.

2019

A Chance Encounter

A rain-soaked evening, a shared umbrella, and a conversation that outlasted the storm. Neither of us planned to fall in love at a bus stop — and yet.

2020

The First Dance

A quiet dinner turned into an impromptu dance in the kitchen. We agreed, without saying it aloud, that this was the beginning of something rare.

2023

The Proposal

Beneath the same rain that brought us together, Julian asked a question he'd been carrying for years. Isabella said yes before he finished it.

2026

Happily Ever After

And now, surrounded by the people who shaped us, we begin the next chapter — one written by both our hands, together.
